Overview

The DeFi Growth Index (DGI) tracks the performance of early-stage DeFi projects that demonstrate innovative mechanisms and established market demand, with strong potential for significant growth in user base, transaction volume, and Total Value Locked (TVL).

Inclusion Criteria

  • Market capitalization between $10 million and $1 billion
  • Listed on the Ethereum blockchain
  • Minimum 180 days of operation with demonstrated growth
  • Available on major DEXs with sufficient trading volume
  • Transparent tokenomics and supply schedule

Rebalancing & Weighting

The index is reviewed quarterly and rebalanced based on market capitalization. Constituents are weighted according to their relative market sizes, ensuring the portfolio reflects the natural distribution of the DeFi growth sector.

Governance

DGI is governed by RSR stakers who participate in decision-making through proposals and voting. Governors oversee asset selection, risk management, and strategic adjustments while earning 50% of TVL and minting fees.
